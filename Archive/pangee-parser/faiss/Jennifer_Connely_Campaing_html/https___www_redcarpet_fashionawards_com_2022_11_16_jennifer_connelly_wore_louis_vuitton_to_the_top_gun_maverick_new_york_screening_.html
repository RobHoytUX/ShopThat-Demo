Jennifer Connelly Wore Louis Vuitton To The ‘Top Gun: Maverick’ New York Screening

Posted by Catherine Kallon

Jennifer Connelly attended a special CAA screening for ‘Top Gun: Maverick’ which was held at the Whitby Hotel Theater on Friday (November 11) in New York City.

The least surprising thing about this outing is that Jennifer Connelly is wearing Louis Vuitton.

The most surprising thing is that she didn’t wear the full Resort 2023 runway look…but then again, looking at those leather metallic gold trousers – which Gemma Chan was brave enough to wear – I wouldn’t have worn them either, so I will not hold it against Jennifer who is the Queen of head-to-toe runway looks.

Thinking about it, there is a slim to moderately high possibility that Jennifer may have been able to make the trousers work.

Instead, she opted to wear her knitted, beaded ruffled runway top with a pair of tailored black trousers.

A selection of chunky gold jewelry, and Louis Vuitton Matchmake pumps – instead of the runway sneakers – complete her look.

This was well-styled, and very much in tune with the informal event.

I know there were some of you who did like Gemma’s full runway outing during Venice Film Festival.

If you didn’t, does Jennifer’s styling improve the look for you?
