Louis Vuitton’s Shifting Celebrity Strategy

The French leather goods giant has found success with a focused approach to dressing celebrities. Now, it’s broadening its strategy to include TikTok stars and Bollywood actresses.
By
  • Lauren Sherman
07 June 2022
BoF PROFESSIONAL

Key insights

  • Louis Vuitton’s focused celebrity strategy has increased social media conversation around its red-carpet appearances, according to Brandwatch.
  • Keys to its success include big budgets, lots of bets on rising stars and designer Nicolas Ghesquière’s relationships with stylists and celebrities.
  • Now, the brand is dressing a broader range of celebrities from TikTok stars to Bollywood actresses.
Under CEO Francesca Bellettini and designer Anthony Vaccarello, Saint Laurent has more than doubled sales in 5 years and is on track to surpass $3 billion in 2022.
