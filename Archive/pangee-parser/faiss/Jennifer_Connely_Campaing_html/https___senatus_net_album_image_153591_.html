Jennifer Connelly for Louis Vuitton Cruise 2023 #LVCruise
By SENATUS Editor | 8 November 2022
First unveiled at the Salk Institute in California, Nicolas Ghesquière’s latest Cruise Collection revolves around the sun, with reflective looks serving as points of contact between the light and wearer.

Embodying Nicolas Ghesquière's neo-futurist vision, Actress and House Ambassador Jennifer Connelly showcases the collection’s nomadic looks in a diverse range of materials that all reverberate light.


As if on a utopian crossing, the collection can be seen to either celebrate the sun or provide protection from it. From looks that evoke a desert climate to metal tones that reflect solar rays, looks explore the contrast between structured constructions and fluid draping.
