Léon Marchand Ambassador for Louis Vuitton 2024

WE ARE LOVING Léon Marchand and so is everyone around the world including everyone in France. Congratulations to House Ambassador Léon Marchand on winning his Paris 2024 Olympic Medals in five events, including four Olympic records he broke in the 200m Individual Medley, 200m Breaststroke, 200m Butterfly, and 400m Individual Medley.

Léon Marchand will be now the face of Louis Vuitton and will now be celebrated throughout France as one of the most highlighted athletes of their country. We can definitely see Leon being a part of the LVMH group for a long time with being in Advertisements for the brand that they own to sitting front row at the next Louis Vuitton runway show. This amazing athlete has his world on a platter right now and he should take full advantage of it.
