    Blackpink’s Lisa Pulls Not One, But Two Archival Louis Vuitton Looks For Her Surprise Frow Appearance

    By Emily Chan
    6 March 2024
    Kristy Sparow/Getty Images

    Blackpink’s Lisa (full name Lalisa Manobal) is known for being a Celine girl. So it came as a surprise when she was spotted arriving at Nicolas Ghesquière’s 10-year anniversary show for Louis Vuitton in Paris on Tuesday night, while sporting one of the creative director’s most memorable archival designs.

    For her front-row appearance, Lisa pulled a pink embellished frock coat from spring/summer 2018 (British Vogue’s Laura Hawkins’s favourite NG X LV collection), which she wore with black leather hotpants, knee-high stockings and sculptural pumps, giving a sultry edge to the look.

    Proving her knowledge of Ghesquière’s oeuvre even further, the K-pop star went into the archives again for the Louis Vuitton after party. This time around, she opted for the colour-block scuba dress that opened Ghesquière’s Resort 2017 collection (my personal favourite), which was shown in Brazil’s Rio de Janeiro ahead of the 2016 Olympics. With the Paris Olympics just months away, it was a fitting choice that shows just how relevant Ghesquière’s past designs still are, years later.

    Lisa was by no means the only A-lister to opt for an archival look for Ghesquière’s anniversary show: long-time rewear champion Cate Blanchett brought back out the silky blue Louis Vuitton top she wore at the 2023 Oscars – this time teaming it with a pair of black leather trousers.

    It just goes to show that you don’t need to wear a new look to make a fashion statement. Could we see more archival pieces from Louis Vuitton at the Oscars on Sunday? Let’s hope so.
