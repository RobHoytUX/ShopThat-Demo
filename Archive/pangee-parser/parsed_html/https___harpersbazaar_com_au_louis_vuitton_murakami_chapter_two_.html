Chapter Two of the Louis Vuitton x Murakami re-edition is here

Featuring none other than Zendaya.
By Dani Maher, in partnership with Louis Vuitton

IN JANUARY, LOUIS Vuitton just about broke the internet with the news that they would be celebrating the 20-year anniversary of their iconic collaboration with Takashi Murakami with a re-edition collection. Now, the excitement has been reinvigorated as the second chapter of the cherished relaunch arrives.

Aligning with the northern hemisphere’s spring, Chapter Two of Louis Vuitton x Murakami fittingly heroes Japan’s cherry blossom season with Murakami’s Cherry Blossom motif from the original collaboration, meticulously rendered upon more than 40 of the Maison’s creations. We’re talking City Bags to silk Carrés, and sneakers to Speedy 25s.


Embodying a joyful optimism and sense of hope, the cheerful pink flowers are printed — using cutting-edge digital technology — across these House staples, extending as far as the Spell on You perfume, whose signature cylindrical packaging and bottle get a whimsical update with a chorus of sakura petals cascading across their façades. Elsewhere, the Marellini Alma BB and one of three different Capucines City Bags get a rosy glow up — transformed with an all-over rose-coloured rendering, matching the signature hue of Murakami’s cherry blossoms.


Bringing the exciting second chapter to life in the campaign is none other than House Ambassador Zendaya, lensed by the renowned Inez and Vinoodh. Wearing pieces from the new collection — including a Capucines Mini and an On The Go in Monogram canvas — she is surrounded by Murakami’s cheerful blossoms, a vision of springtime joy.

Chapter Two of Louis Vuitton x Murakamia will be available via digital prelaunch & online pre-order on March 14 and in-store March 21. Discover the collection here.
